Need Help with Email Verification?

We’re here to assist you! If you’re having trouble verifying your email address, please follow these steps:

1. Check Your Email Inbox
  • Verify Receipt: Make sure you’ve received the verification email from us. It may take a few minutes to arrive.
  • Spam/Junk Folder: Sometimes our emails can end up in your spam or junk folder. Please check there if you don’t see it in your inbox.
2. Resend Verification Email

If you haven’t received the verification email, you can request a new one by clicking the button below:

Resend Verification Email
3. Ensure Your Email Address is Correct

Double-check that the email address you provided is correct. If you believe there might be a mistake, please contact support.

4. Contact Support

If you’ve tried the above steps and still need assistance, our support team is here to help. Please provide the following information when you reach out:

  • Your Email Address: The one you used to register.
  • Description of the Issue: Any error messages or problems you’re encountering.

FAQs
How long does email verification take?
Verification emails are usually sent within a few minutes. If you don’t receive it, check your spam folder or request a resend.
What should I do if my email address is incorrect?
If you realize there was a mistake with your email address, please contact support to update it.
Can I verify my email address on a different device?
Yes, you can verify your email address from any device as long as you have access to your email account.
